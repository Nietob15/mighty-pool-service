About us

Who we are

We are a friendly and professional pool services & renovation company

Mighty Pool Services is proud to serve all of our local San Diego customers, and we strive to make every customer who requires proper pool maintenance smile. We are a high-quality swimming pool maintenance company. A pool is only as good as the equipment used to keep it clean and operational. Our extensive work experience with pooling systems, skimming surfaces, brushed pools, and pool pump maintenance has made us the one-of-a-kind team that we are. Our Protocol

In order to keep your pool clean, clear, and operational.

Performing a thorough cleaning

Our pool service technicians have been thoroughly trained to keep your pool clean! Brushing the pool's surface, walls, and waterline tile is essential for preventing stains on the interior finish and keeping algae out of the pool.

Dependability.

All of our pool care technicians have been trained to do one thing... look after your pool! You can count on our staff to provide excellent customer service, going above and beyond to ensure that your pool is operational and performing to its full potential. Nothing is too difficult for us to accomplish for our client!

Communication

This is an important aspect of our company that we work on every day! You can count on prompt, clear, and concise communication. During each visit, we collect and submit pool data and photographs. All information is immediately shared and classified for easy reference.

Implementing chemicals correctly

The chemicals we use are the most effective and pure chemicals available for treating and maintaining your pool.
